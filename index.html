About Me

Bio: I am a rising junior at Claremont McKenna College planning to complete a dual major in International Relations and Chinese, along with a sequence (minor) in Computer Science. Being from California, I had the privilege of growing up in a rich multicultural environment, which sparked my interest in other cultures and languages and led me to a dual major in International Relations and Chinese. However, after taking a class in Python and getting some first-hand experience using coding to better my own life, I also developed an interest in computer science. At the moment, I am exploring ways to weave my varied interests into what I do, hoping to find a career that will allow me to use both my humanities background and passion for computer science. I am looking for internships or part-time jobs that will allow me to further my coding skills.
